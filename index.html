About the Game

You are a newly appointed lord of a small castle. Manage your castle's finances, hire troops, resolve conflicts between peasants, and bring order to the kingdom. In this text-based game, you will need to survive the schemes of your enemies in your quest.
Will you stay loyal to the King, or will you join the incoming rebellion to overthrow him and establish a council of Lords?

Features

Visual Novel Game

Players experience the story by advancing through text dialogues and some static images.

Choice-driven Narrative

Players can immerse themselves in fantasy adventures, manage a castle's ressources and shape how the story unfolds.

Customization Features

Players have the ability to tailor the experience to their preferences (name the character, choose between four beginnings...)

Exploration and Imagination

Choices offers players the ability to explore other lives and even worlds, just like they would by reading a book or watching a TV drama. This is the main appeal of such games.

Responsive Layout

Players have access to various tools like music, login, live view of characteristics, animations, etc.. to further enhance the playing experience.

About Us

Duke of Cornwall is an interactive novel with an eye-catching GUI made by TEK-UP students where your choices control the story. It's entirely text-based, fueled by the vast, unstoppable power of your imagination.

Call to Action Section

Build your power through war, diplomacy or stealth. It's up to you to decide your rise or fall in Duke of Cornwall

Characters

You can choose between four possible character paths, to ensure more complexity, multiple interesting choices and lots of tactical interactions.

The Commoner

Coming from the bottom, this character has no roots, riches or relations. Will you survive as a commoner to rule over Cornwall, possibly over the whole kingdom?

The Noble

Rich, powerful, this character has everything he needs to establish his rule over Cornwall. Born a noble, you are not particularly liked by most people. Will you forge new alliances and take-over as a King, perhaps die trying?

The Diplomat

An old friend of the King, you are particularly liked in his court. You helped him survive and thrive through the rebellion. This makes you untrustworthy in the eyes of the other Dukes. Will you survive their schemes?

The Knight

A man of his word, this strong and disciplined character is quite feared and respected. No one particularly trusts your mysterious persona. Will you, as a former Knight, form a huge army and take-over the throne?
